About Us

India and the world have experienced rapid changes during the last several decades. The term “development” has acquired now importance as well as multiple interpretations. Alternative development paradigms and strategies are discussed passionately at home and abroad. New problems and challenges have emerged and they have no clear answers.

There is a need to cater to such a need for promoting systematic research and development in critical areas related to multifarious issues in environmental conservation, community empowerment and sustainable development, EcoDEVA India is a serious developmental research and NGO initiative in India with a mission to work for promoting environment centered development at grass-roots levels of community.

Eco Initiatives

Eco Livelihoods

Eco-livelihoods initiative aims in building rural livelihoods and sustainability issues in rural areas through micro-finance and micro-entrepreneurship.

Eco Stove

Low cost biomass stoves that promise to revolutionize rural health care, significantly reduce deforestation and even curb global warming.

Eco Store

A place to make a variety of green products available to communities and make them environmentally conscious people.

Eco Furniture

Recycled furniture made from sustainable or reclaimed materials is an eco-friendly way to decorate a home in a green way.
